Hi there, I’m Ricardo, a curious 30-something husband and proud father to an amazing two-year-old daughter. When I’m not chasing after my toddler or enjoying nature with my loved ones, I’m often found immersed deep into the world of data analysis, unraveling its complexities and discovering new insights.

My journey into data analytics began with an unpredictable combination of opportunity, luck, and determination. Armed with a bachelor's degree in Petroleum Engineering from Texas Tech University, I embarked on my career as a field engineer, working from within an operations vehicle known as a "data van." From this unconventional vantage point, I meticulously monitored live data streams, transforming complex information from depths of 5,000-10,000 feet underground into insightful charts and reports. I played a crucial part in ensuring both operational efficiency and safety within the hazardous oilfield environment.

An unexpected opportunity led me to transition into the dynamic world of startups. Here, I co-founded a social-media e-commerce platform and assumed the role of COO, in charge of all aspects of data analysis for decision-making. This encompassed integrating financial, marketing, user acquisition and engagement, technology, and regulatory considerations, ensuring that every strategic move was anchored in data-driven insights.

As one of the founders, I gained firsthand experience in the challenges of building a business from the ground up. I developed a profound appreciation for the power of data, a guiding force that informed every strategic decision and propelled our pursuit for innovation and growth. Data serves as the compass guiding a ship through uncharted waters, offering clarity and direction amidst turbulent conditions.
